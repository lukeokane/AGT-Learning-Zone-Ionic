Privacy Policy for The Learning Zone

At The Learning Zone, one of our main priorities is the privacy of all users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by The Learning Zone and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through email at itlc@dkit.ie.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

We are a Data Controller of your information.

The Learning Zone legal basis for collecting and using the personal information described in this Privacy Policy depends on the Personal Information we collect and the specific context in which we collect the information:

  • The Learning Zone needs to perform a contract with you
  • You have given The Learning Zone permission to do so
  • Processing your personal information is in The Learning Zone legitimate interests
  • The Learning Zone needs to comply with the law

The Learning Zone will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. If you wish to be informed what Personal Information we hold about you and if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

In certain circumstances, you have the following data protection rights:

  • The right to access, update or to delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object.
  • The right of restriction.
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

Data Storage

The Learning Zone stores data within a database which is securly hosted by Dundalk Institute of Technology.

Sensitive data that can be used to identify a person includes and is not limited to:
  • Full name.
  • Email address.
  • Attending course and year in course (DkIT students).
  • Records of physical attendance at bookings/events

Why we need your data

This information is required for the mandatory functionality of The Learning Zone to occur. This is also has the effect of personalizing a person's experience when interacting and receiving correspondance from The Learning Zone.

Data Transfer

All data to and from a person is always transfered using encryption algorithms and data integrity checks. Data is secured using industry standard Transport Layer Security protocols.

Email communication

Email communication is used strictly in the The Learning Zone to communicate information relevant to particular activities a person has consented to participating in. These activities include signing up to bookings and requesting bookings. A person will not receive emails promoting or advocating services that are not relevant to their direct and understood actions with The Learning Zone. Email communication is always transfered using encryption algorithms and data integrity checks. Communication is secured using industry standard Transport Layer Security protocols.

Log Files

The Learning Zone follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they use applications. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site and tracking users' movement in the application.
